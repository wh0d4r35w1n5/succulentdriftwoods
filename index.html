The Experience

Succulent Driftwood Garden Workshops

Step into a sun-dappled creative space where driftwood, succulents and coastal calm come together. You'll design and build a living sculpture — a one-of-a-kind piece of Byron Bay to take home.

Choose your own unique piece of Byron Bay to take home with you.

Whether you're a total beginner or a seasoned maker, Linda guides you through every step with warmth, expertise and a genuine love for the craft. It's meditative, joyful, and deeply satisfying.

Everything you need

What's included

  • All materials supplied - Driftwood, succulents, soil, tools — everything is ready and waiting. Just bring yourself.
  • Your unique piece - Choose from a selection of driftwood and plants to create something that feels unmistakably yours — and Byron.
  • Morning tea - Settle in with a relaxed morning tea as you create. Slow down, connect, enjoy.
  • Knowledge to keep creating - Leave with care tips and creative confidence to keep making long after the workshop ends.
